Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple sound design projects may take 1-2 weeks, while full film scores can take 1-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with independent filmmakers?

Absolutely! We love working with independent creators and offer flexible pricing structures. We believe great stories deserve great sound, regardless of budget size.

Can you work remotely?

Yes, we work with clients globally. We have robust remote collaboration workflows and can provide high-quality audio services regardless of location.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in all industry-standard formats including WAV, AIFF, MP3, and specialized formats for games and installations. We'll match your technical requirements exactly.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes, we include revision rounds in our project scope. We work collaboratively to ensure the final product exceeds your expectations.

What about licensing and rights?

We provide clear licensing terms based on your project needs. From exclusive buyouts to usage-based licensing, we'll structure a deal that works for both parties.
